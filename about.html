About Us

Smil Resort is a small, family-run resort located in the heart of Staro Nagoricane, a small town in the southeastern part of Macedonia.
Started construction in 1986, the resort was opened in 1989 and has been welcoming guests ever since.
The resort is surrounded by beautiful nature, with fields of cotton, wheat and sunflowers stretching as far as the eye can see.
The resort is owned and operated by the family of Smiljkovikj, who have been working in the tourism industry for over 30 years.
